Ham Salad

How to Make Ham Salad

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the
recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 3 cups ground fully cooked ham
  • 2 hard cooked eggs, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped celery
  • 4 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped onion
  • 1 cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Mix ham, eggs, celery, pickle relish, and onion together in a medium bowl until combined.
  2. Mix mayonnaise and mustard together in a small bowl
  3. add to ham mixture and stir to coat.
  4. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
