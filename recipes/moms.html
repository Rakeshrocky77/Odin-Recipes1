Mom's Potato Latkes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded potato
  • ¼ cup grated onion
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 6 saltine crackers, or as needed, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Mix potato, onion, eggs, crushed crackers, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  2. Cook in batches in the hot oil until browned and crisp, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Drain latkes on a paper towel-line plate.
  3. Heat 1/4 inch oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the potato mixture, first pressing potato mixture against the side of the bowl to remove excess liquid, into the hot oil; slightly flatten the latkes into the oil with the back of your spoon so they are an even thickness.
